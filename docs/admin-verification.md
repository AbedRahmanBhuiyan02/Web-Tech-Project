# Admin Verification Checklist

- Log in with an admin account and open the dashboard.
- Confirm dashboard cards show medicine, category, customer, and pending order totals.
- Create, edit, and delete categories, including the protected delete case when medicines exist.
- Create, edit, upload an image for, and delete medicines, including the protected delete case when a pending order exists.
- Delete a customer and confirm related cart, orders, order items, and payments are removed through database cascades.
- Open purchase requests and confirm customer name, address, total, date, and status are visible.
- Accept and reject pending purchase requests through the AJAX status buttons.
- Open purchase history and confirm accepted orders show customer details, medicines, quantities, and totals.
